* Built a highly **interactive dashboard** in Looker Studio.
* Key Visualizations: Sales trends, Top Categories by Revenue/Volume, and geographic distribution by city.
* Implemented interactive filters for segmentation by city, category, and time.

### 3. Business Insights & Recommendations
* **Promotional Focus:** Recommended targeting **eBooks** (due to high volume) and **Robots** (due to highest revenue).
* **Geographic Optimization:** Recommended optimizing promotion or campaign in key cities: **Washington**, **Houston**, and **San Diego**.
* **Timing Strategy:** Advised initiating promotional campaigns earlier, leading up to the end of the year, based on seasonality trends.
* **Low-Performer Evaluation:** Provided insights for evaluating and addressing low-performing categories.
